Card Captor Sakura

So Card Captor Sakura was something that I'd watch when ever I ran into it and I only have a few memories of watching it. I liked it but it never really captivated me like other anime did. I never realized that the English version named just Cardcaptors is what I probably saw and it was reedited so badly it completely changed the story. They execs tried to make Li the main character of the show when it completely follows Sakura and he's to a degree a rival of hers. Much later on I was able to watch the whole show and I could tell that it was so good. I really think it's one of the most well animated shows around and the artwork is exceptional.
Now if you're going to watch it, I'd recommend ignoring some of the relationships because they are... weird. At least all the straight ones. Aside from that, the show is just hella cute and most of the show is just laid back where some times 95% of it is just Sakura chilling with her friends cooking or something then the last 2 minutes they remember they have to fight some monster.
